Welcome

In this project we will create consciousness about how expensive having a correct diet is

What is a healthy diet?

A healthy diet is a diet that provide us with levels of nutrients that the body occupies for the maintenance and regeneration of the body, and low levels of foods harmful to health.

Adequate

You consume the nutrients your body needs

Balanced

You consume different types of food and you dont eat too much of one type

Complete

You dont miss any type of nutrients

Enough

You eat enough food and it depends of on how much calories your body consumes

Variated

At least you need on a dish 5 differents types of food

Why is it so expensive?

Why? After having purchased some food and condiments necessary to continue with a healthy lifestyle, on average the monthly cost of eating healthy is 6 thousand pesos
